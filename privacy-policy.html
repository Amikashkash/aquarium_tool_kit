Privacy Policy for Aquarium Tool Kit

This privacy policy describes how Aquarium Tool Kit uses and protects user data. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

No Collection of Personal Data

We want to assure you that Aquarium Tool Kit does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) or any other user data. This includes, but is not limited to, names, addresses, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or any other information that could be used to identify you.

Local Storage of User-Created Data

The App allows you to create and store custom data within the App. This data is stored exclusively on your device's internal storage and is not transmitted to us or any third parties. This data is only accessible by the App itself.

The user created data may include, but is not limited to: custom addetive list.

Data Deletion

You can delete the data stored by the App in the following ways:

  • **Clearing App Data:** By clearing the App's data through your device's settings, you will delete all data stored by the App.
  • **Uninstalling the App:** Uninstalling the App will also delete all data associated with it.
  • **delete from within the app:** there is an option inside the app to delete any data you entered
